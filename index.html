About Us

Shah Overseas is an energetic professional firm that counsels students who desire to study abroad. It has roots in Nagpur & San Jose, USA. There's mystery to studying in the US universities, and we have demystified it with our step-by-step service that will see you through every phase of the application process—from planning and GRE® prep, University admission to all the way up to your landing a job in the USA. Our students have been successful in securing admissions at Ivy League USA universities. We do our own research and after a detailed understanding of a student's profile and analysis of their goals and aspirations, we research, shortlist and recommend a choice of program, school or University to suit each student.

    GRE:

    Whether you are planning to go to graduate school or business school — or just exploring your options — you only need to take the one test accepted by thousands of graduate and business schools worldwide the GRE® General Test. GRE® test scores are valid for five years. In India, the computer-delivered test is available on a continuous basis throughout the year. Shah Overseas has one of the best faculties for coaching and has developed advanced software for mimicking real GRE conditions.


    ScoreSelect® Option:

    With the GRE General Test, you decide which scores to send to schools. If you feel you didn't do your best on test day, that's okay. You can retake the test and then send only the scores you want schools to see.

    University Selection & Application

    University Selection – Your Second Home

    While selecting universities for you we consider factors like location, cost, reputation, competitiveness, and whether the school is public or private. We discuss short listed universities with you and your parents. After your consent we start application for the universities. We don't try to match you to the school, but rather find the school that matches you and your priorities and long-term goals. Remember that no official ranking system exists for colleges and universities in the United States. The best college or university is the one that is best for you and meets your requirements—academic, financial, and personal.

    For grad students, the academic experience is paramount, and your career goals will impact what you look for in a program. If you want a master's degree to give you that professional edge, then the overall quality of the faculty may be more important than finding the ideal mentor. If you are aiming for a PhD, however, finding a specific professor to serve as your thesis advisor trumps the talents of the faculty at large. Apply to schools that have one or more professors who do research in your general area of interest, and with whom you can imagine working closely for a year (or six).
